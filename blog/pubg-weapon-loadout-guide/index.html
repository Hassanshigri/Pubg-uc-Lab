PUBG Weapon Loadout Guide: Best Builds for 2025

PUBG Expert March 1, 2025 7 min read
Master PUBG Mobile with our 2025 Weapon Loadout Guide. Discover the best weapons, attachments, and strategies for dominating close-range, mid-range, and long-range combat scenarios.

Why Loadouts Matter in PUBG Mobile

In PUBG Mobile, choosing the right weapons and attachments can make the difference between a Chicken Dinner and an early exit. With over 40 weapons available, each with unique strengths and weaknesses, building the perfect loadout for your playstyle is crucial. This guide breaks down the best weapons and attachments for 2025, tailored for various combat ranges and scenarios.[](https://www.gamersdecide.com/articles/pubg-best-loadouts)

Assault Rifles: The Backbone of Any Loadout

Assault rifles (ARs) are versatile weapons that excel in most combat situations. Here are the top picks for 2025:

  • M416: Known for its low recoil and versatility, the M416 is a favorite for all skill levels. Equip with a Compensator, Vertical Foregrip, Extended QuickDraw Mag, and a 4x ACOG Scope for mid-range dominance.
    • [](https://skycoach.gg/blog/pubg-battlegrounds/articles/pubg-weapons-tier-list)
  • Beryl M762: A high-damage AR with aggressive recoil, perfect for skilled players. Use a Compensator, Vertical Foregrip, and Red Dot Sight for close to mid-range fights.
    • [](https://www.razzem.com/whats-the-pubg-weapon-meta-in-2025/)
  • AUG: Recently buffed, the AUG offers low recoil and high bullet velocity. Pair with a 4x Scope and Compensator for a balanced loadout.
    • [](https://www.razzem.com/whats-the-pubg-weapon-meta-in-2025/)

Designated Marksman Rifles (DMRs): Precision at Range

DMRs are ideal for mid-to-long-range engagements, offering a balance of power and fire rate:

  • Mk12: A top-tier DMR with minimal recoil and high fire rate. Equip with a 6x Scope, Compensator, and Extended QuickDraw Mag for precision sniping.
    • [](https://skycoach.gg/blog/pubg-battlegrounds/articles/pubg-weapons-tier-list)
  • SLR: High damage but challenging recoil. Use a Compensator, Cheek Pad, and 6x Scope for long-range accuracy.
    • [](https://1v9.gg/blog/pubg-mobile-battlegrounds-best-weapons-attachments-tierlist-ranked)
  • Mini14: Forgiving with low recoil and high bullet velocity, perfect for beginners. Pair with an 8x Scope and Extended Mag.
    • [](https://www.gamersdecide.com/articles/pubg-best-loadouts)

Sniper Rifles: Long-Range Lethality

For players who prefer long-range engagements, these sniper rifles deliver devastating power:

  • AWM: The ultimate sniper rifle, capable of one-shot kills against Level 3 helmets. Found in care packages, equip with a Suppressor and 8x Scope.
    • [](https://1v9.gg/blog/pubg-mobile-battlegrounds-best-weapons-attachments-tierlist-ranked)
  • M24: A reliable bolt-action rifle with high damage. Use a Suppressor, Cheek Pad, and 8x Scope for consistent performance.
    • [](https://overgear.com/guides/pubg/best-weapons-and-attachments/)
  • Kar98k: Widely available and capable of one-shot headshots against Level 2 helmets. Equip with an 8x Scope and Cheek Pad.
    • [](https://skycoach.gg/blog/pubg-battlegrounds/articles/pubg-weapons-tier-list)

Submachine Guns (SMGs): Close-Quarters Kings

SMGs shine in close-range combat, offering high fire rates and low recoil:

  • P90: A crate-exclusive SMG with a 50-round magazine and minimal recoil. Equip with its built-in flip-up sights for versatility.
    • [](https://overgear.com/guides/pubg/best-weapons-and-attachments/)
  • UMP45: Reliable with great damage and control. Use a Red Dot Sight, Suppressor, and Extended QuickDraw Mag.
    • [](https://www.razzem.com/whats-the-pubg-weapon-meta-in-2025/)
  • MP9: Fast-firing and stable, ideal for early-game fights. Pair with a Red Dot Sight and Extended Mag.
    • [](https://www.razzem.com/whats-the-pubg-weapon-meta-in-2025/)

Shotguns: Devastating Close-Range Power

Shotguns are perfect for clearing buildings and tight spaces:

  • DBS: A crate-exclusive shotgun with high burst damage. Equip with a 4x Scope for surprising mid-range potential.
    • [](https://www.gamersdecide.com/articles/pubg-best-loadouts)
  • S1897: A pump-action shotgun that hits hard. Use a Choke to extend its effective range.
    • [](https://segmentnext.com/playerunknowns-battlegrounds-best-loadouts-guide/)

Recommended Loadouts by Playstyle

Here are tailored loadouts for different playstyles in PUBG Mobile:

  • Assault Loadout (Versatile): M416 (4x Scope, Compensator, Vertical Foregrip) + Mini14 (8x Scope, Extended Mag). Ideal for mid-range fights with long-range backup.
    • [](https://pubg.fandom.com/wiki/Best_Loadouts_for_PLAYERUNKNOWN%2527s_BATTLEGROUNDS)
  • Close-Quarters Loadout: P90 (Built-in Sights) + DBS (4x Scope). Perfect for aggressive pushes in Sanhok or urban areas.
    • [](https://1v9.gg/blog/pubg-mobile-battlegrounds-best-weapons-attachments-tierlist-ranked)
  • Ranged Loadout: AWM (8x Scope, Suppressor) + Beryl M762 (Red Dot, Compensator). Snipe from afar and handle close threats.
    • [](https://1v9.gg/blog/pubg-mobile-battlegrounds-best-weapons-attachments-tierlist-ranked)

Essential Attachments

Attachments can make or break your loadout. Prioritize these:

  • Compensator: Reduces recoil for ARs, DMRs, and snipers.
  • Vertical Foregrip: Improves vertical recoil control for ARs and SMGs.
  • Extended QuickDraw Mag: Increases magazine size and reload speed.
  • Scopes (4x, 6x, 8x): Essential for mid-to-long-range accuracy.
  • Suppressor: Reduces noise for stealthy engagements.

Pro Tips for Building Loadouts

Maximize your performance with these expert tips:

  • Match your loadout to the map: Use SMGs on Sanhok, DMRs on Miramar.
  • Prioritize attachments early: A fully kitted M416 outperforms a bare AWM.
  • Carry a Pan: It protects your back from bullets and is a reliable melee weapon.
    • [](https://www.razzem.com/whats-the-pubg-weapon-meta-in-2025/)
  • Balance ammo types: Pair weapons with different ammo (e.g., 5.56mm and 7.62mm) to avoid shortages.
  • Practice recoil control in training mode to master high-damage weapons like the Beryl.

Pro Tip

Test your loadouts in PUBG Mobile’s training mode to perfect your aim and recoil control before jumping into ranked matches.

Conclusion

Building the perfect loadout in PUBG Mobile requires understanding your playstyle, the map, and the current meta. Whether you prefer spraying with an M416, sniping with an AWM, or dominating close quarters with a P90, this guide provides the tools to succeed. Stay updated with patches, as the weapon meta evolves, and practice your loadouts to secure more Chicken Dinners![](https://www.lagofast.com/en/blog/pubg-weapon-tier-list/)
