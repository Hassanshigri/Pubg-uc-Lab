Pro Strategies: How to Win More Chicken Dinners

Pro Player January 12, 2025 7 min read
Master the art of survival with these proven strategies from top PUBG players. Learn positioning, timing, and tactical decisions that will increase your win rate significantly.

The Foundation: Understanding Core Mechanics

Before diving into advanced strategies, it's crucial to master the fundamental mechanics of PUBG. These basics form the foundation upon which all advanced tactics are built.

Map Knowledge and Positioning

One of the most critical aspects of PUBG success is understanding map layouts and strategic positioning. Every map has unique characteristics that influence gameplay.

Circle Management

The shrinking play zone is your biggest enemy and ally. Understanding circle mechanics can make the difference between survival and elimination.

Early Game Strategies (0-15 minutes)

The early game sets the foundation for your entire match. Making smart decisions here can give you a significant advantage over other players.

Drop Zone Selection

Choosing where to land is your first strategic decision. Consider hot drops for high-risk, high-reward gameplay or safe drops for peaceful looting.

Looting Priority

Time is precious in PUBG. Prioritize your looting: weapons first, then armor, medical items, attachments, and utility items.

Mid Game Tactics (15-30 minutes)

The mid-game is where most players are eliminated. This phase requires careful positioning and smart engagement decisions.

Engagement Rules

Not every enemy needs to be engaged. Pick your battles wisely, look for third-party opportunities, and know when to disengage.

Positioning and Rotation

Smart positioning can win games without firing a shot. Use natural cover, maintain high ground, and keep escape routes open.

Late Game Mastery (30+ minutes)

The final circles are where legends are made. This is where your strategic thinking and mechanical skills are put to the ultimate test.

Final Circle Strategies

Stay near the circle edge to avoid being surrounded, use sound management, and save utility items for critical moments.

1v1 Situations

When it's down to the final two players, use sound to locate your opponent, keep moving, and stay calm under pressure.

Advanced Combat Techniques

Beyond basic positioning, mastering advanced combat techniques can give you the edge in critical situations.

Peeking and Pre-aiming

Use right-side peeking when possible, pre-aim common angles, and use lean mechanics to minimize exposure.

Movement and Strafing

Never stand still in the open, use unpredictable movement patterns, and combine crouching with movement.

Pro Tip

Practice these strategies in training mode and casual matches before using them in ranked games. Muscle memory and decision-making speed improve with repetition.

Squad Play Strategies

Playing with a team requires coordination and different tactics than solo play.

Communication is Key

Use clear, concise callouts, call out enemy positions with compass directions, and coordinate movements and attacks.

Role Specialization

Each squad member should have a specific role: IGL for strategic decisions, entry fragger for engagements, support player for covering fire, and lurker for flanking.

Mental Game and Psychology

PUBG is as much a mental game as it is a mechanical one. Your mindset and decision-making under pressure are crucial.

Staying Calm Under Pressure

Take deep breaths to stay calm, focus on the present moment, trust your training and instincts, and don't let previous mistakes affect current decisions.

Learning from Defeats

Every loss is an opportunity to improve. Review your gameplay, focus on one aspect to improve at a time, and celebrate small improvements.

Practice and Improvement

Consistent improvement requires dedicated practice and analysis.

Training Regimen

Spend time in training mode practicing aim, review your replays, watch professional players, and practice specific scenarios repeatedly.

Setting Realistic Goals

Set specific, measurable goals, focus on process over results, track your progress over time, and be patient with your development.

Conclusion

Winning in PUBG requires a combination of mechanical skill, strategic thinking, and mental fortitude. While these strategies won't make you a pro overnight, consistent application and practice will significantly improve your win rate over time.

Remember, every pro player started as a beginner. Focus on improving one aspect at a time, stay patient with your progress, and most importantly, enjoy the journey of becoming a better PUBG player.
