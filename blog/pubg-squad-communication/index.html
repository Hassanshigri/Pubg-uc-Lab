PUBG Squad Communication Guide: Win as a Team in 2025

PUBG Expert April 5, 2025 6 min read
Effective squad communication is the key to dominating PUBG Mobile in 2025. Learn the best callouts, strategies, and tips to coordinate with your team and secure those Chicken Dinners.

Why Squad Communication Matters

In PUBG Mobile, squad play is where teamwork shines. Clear, concise, and strategic communication can turn a good team into a great one, helping you outmaneuver opponents and survive the shrinking playzone. This guide covers essential communication techniques to elevate your squad’s performance in 2025.

Setting Up for Success

Before diving into a match, ensure your squad is ready to communicate effectively:

  • Use Voice Chat: Enable in-game voice chat or use platforms like Discord for clearer audio quality.
  • Assign Roles: Designate a squad leader for decision-making, a scout for recon, and support players for healing or covering fire.
  • Test Equipment: Check microphones and headsets to avoid mid-game disruptions.
  • Agree on Callout Terms: Standardize terms like “north,” “compound,” or “two-story” for clarity.

Effective Callouts: Be Clear and Concise

Good callouts provide critical information quickly. Follow these principles:

  • Location: Use compass directions (e.g., “Enemy at 270, behind the rock”) or landmarks (e.g., “Enemy in the red barn”).
  • Enemy Details: Specify numbers and status (e.g., “Two enemies, one knocked at 180”).
  • Action Requests: Clearly state needs (e.g., “Need smoke at blue house” or “Cover me, I’m pushing”).
  • Avoid Clutter: Keep comms focused—avoid unnecessary chatter during intense moments.

Map-Specific Communication Strategies

Different maps require tailored communication approaches:

  • Erangel: Focus on vehicle callouts (e.g., “UAZ moving north at School”) due to open terrain.
  • Sanhok: Emphasize close-range enemy positions (e.g., “Enemy in bushes, 50 meters east”) for fast-paced fights.
  • Miramar: Use long-range callouts (e.g., “Sniper on hill at 320, 200 meters”) for distant engagements.
  • Arctic Wilderness: Call out weather changes (e.g., “Blizzard incoming, move to ice cave”) to adapt to dynamic conditions.

Team Roles and Coordination

Assigning roles enhances squad synergy. Common roles include:

  • In-Game Leader (IGL): Makes strategic calls like rotations, pushes, or retreats.
  • Fragger: Leads aggressive plays and engages enemies first.
  • Support: Provides healing, smokes, or covering fire.
  • Scout/Sniper: Gathers intel or takes long-range shots.

Using In-Game Tools

PUBG Mobile’s 2025 updates enhance communication tools:

  • Quick Chat Commands: Use pre-set messages (e.g., “Enemy spotted!”) for fast communication without voice.
  • Map Pings: Mark enemy locations or loot directly on the map for visual clarity.
  • Improved Voice Chat: Leverage Season 25’s enhanced voice quality for clearer comms.
  • Clan System: Use clan chat for pre-match planning and post-match debriefs.

Handling High-Pressure Situations

In intense moments, communication can break down. Stay calm with these tips:

  • Prioritize Key Info: Focus on enemy positions and immediate threats (e.g., “Three enemies pushing from south, need backup”).
  • Stay Calm: Avoid yelling to keep comms clear.
  • Revive Coordination: Call out when reviving (e.g., “Reviving at blue house, cover me”) and use smokes for safety.
  • Endgame Strategy: Plan final-circle rotations early (e.g., “Let’s hold the ridge at 090”).

Pro Tips for Squad Communication

Elevate your squad’s performance with these expert tips:

  • Practice callouts in training mode to build muscle memory.
  • Record matches to review communication breakdowns and improve.
  • Use short, standardized terms (e.g., “knocked” instead of “downed”) to avoid confusion.
  • Rotate roles occasionally to understand each teammate’s perspective.
  • Join PUBG UCLab’s Discord for squad-finding and strategy discussions.

Pro Tip

During intense firefights, designate one player to make final calls to avoid conflicting instructions and maintain squad focus.

Conclusion

Effective squad communication is the cornerstone of success in PUBG Mobile. By mastering clear callouts, assigning roles, and leveraging in-game tools, your team can outsmart and outplay opponents. Whether you’re pushing for rank or aiming for fun, these strategies will help you secure more Chicken Dinners in 2025.
