PUBG Mobile 2.0 Preview: What’s Coming in 2025

PUBG Expert May 10, 2025 6 min read
PUBG Mobile is gearing up for a massive 2.0 update in 2025, promising new maps, gameplay mechanics, collaborations, and enhanced anti-cheat systems. Get a sneak peek at what’s coming and how it’ll transform your battle royale experience.

What is PUBG Mobile 2.0?

The PUBG Mobile 2.0 update is set to be a major overhaul, introducing innovative features and refreshing the battle royale experience for 2025. Following the success of previous updates, this version aims to enhance gameplay, visuals, and fairness while keeping the core PUBG Mobile experience intact. Here’s what to expect based on the latest teasers and community buzz.

New Map: Urban Abyss

A brand-new map, tentatively called Urban Abyss, is rumored to debut with PUBG Mobile 2.0. This map blends dense cityscapes with underground tunnels, offering a mix of close-quarters combat and strategic positioning. Key features include:

  • Dynamic Environments: Collapsible buildings and interactive elements like destructible walls.
  • Vertical Gameplay: Rooftop vantage points and ziplines for fast traversal.
  • Night Mode: A darker, neon-lit aesthetic with enhanced visibility challenges.

New Gameplay Mechanics

PUBG Mobile 2.0 introduces innovative mechanics to keep matches fresh and engaging:

  • Cosmic Hoverboard: A new vehicle inspired by skateboarding, allowing players to perform tricks and navigate terrain quickly.
    • [](https://x.com/PUBGMOBILE/status/1953550591663182208)
  • Advanced Supply Zones: High-risk, high-reward areas with premium loot, spawning randomly near the flight path.
  • Dynamic Weather: Maps like Arctic Wilderness will feature blizzards and fog, impacting visibility and strategy.
  • Recall Towers 2.0: Upgraded towers allow squad revives in ranked modes with shorter cooldowns.

New Weapons and Upgrades

The update introduces new firepower and customization options:

  • Dravion X-Suit AWM: An upgradable AWM with unique animations, exclusive to the Dravion X-Suit.
    • [](https://x.com/PUBGMOBILE/status/1953637420156551172)
  • XT Weapon Packs: Upgrade select weapons to their XT variants at Supply Shops for enhanced damage and recoil control.
  • Smart Grenades: Throwable explosives that can lock onto vehicles or enemies for precise strikes.

Exciting Collaborations

PUBG Mobile is known for its high-profile collaborations, and 2.0 is no exception:

  • Anime Crossover: A rumored collaboration with a major anime franchise, featuring themed skins and a limited-time mode with iconic characters.
  • Music Partnership: Exclusive emotes and lobby music inspired by a global pop sensation, similar to past BLACKPINK collabs.
  • World of Wonder 1.0: A creative mode with urban city blocks for players to design custom maps and modes.

Anti-Cheat and Performance Upgrades

Fair play and smooth performance are priorities for 2025:

  • Ban Pan 3.0: Enhanced anti-cheat system with real-time monitoring and faster ban enforcement for hacks like X-Ray vision.
  • Fog of War 2.0: Improved server-side data filtering to prevent wall hacks and aimbots.
  • Optimized Performance: Reduced lag, faster load times, and haptic feedback for weapons and vehicles on supported devices.

Royale Pass and Rewards

The update kicks off a new Royale Pass with exclusive rewards:

  • Mythic Outfits: New sets like Stellar Guardian and Cyber Phantom, available via the Royale Pass.
  • Seasonal Challenges: Missions tied to new mechanics, offering skins, emotes, and UC rewards.
  • Progression Boost: Faster tier progression with fewer Bronze and Silver levels for quicker access to ranked rewards.

Expected Release and Download Details

While an official release date hasn’t been confirmed, PUBG Mobile 2.0 is expected to roll out in mid-2025, likely around June. Here’s what you need to know:

  • Availability: The update will be available on Google Play Store, App Store, and via APK for Android users.
  • File Size: Approximately 1.8 GB on Android and 2.5 GB on iOS, so ensure you have enough storage.
  • System Requirements: Android 5.1.1 or above with 3 GB RAM; iOS 10.0 or later.

Pro Tips for Preparing for 2.0

Get ready for the update with these tips:

  • Clear storage space on your device to accommodate the update’s large file size.
  • Practice in training mode to master new weapons and vehicles like the Cosmic Hoverboard.
  • Join PUBG UCLab’s Discord to stay updated on patch notes and community events.
  • Save UC for the new Royale Pass to unlock exclusive rewards early.
  • Test your internet connection for optimal performance during the update rollout.

Pro Tip

Follow PUBG Mobile’s official social media and PUBG UCLab for early access to beta testing opportunities, giving you a head start on mastering the new features.

Conclusion

PUBG Mobile 2.0 for 2025 promises to elevate the battle royale experience with a new map, innovative mechanics, exciting collaborations, and robust anti-cheat measures. Whether you’re a casual player or a ranked grinder, this update offers something for everyone. Stay tuned for the official release and dive into the action!
